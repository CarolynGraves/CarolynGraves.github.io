Q1. What is a VPN? A1. A VPN (Virtual Private Network) is a type of network that allows users to access the Internet securely and anonymously by creating a private network from public Internet connections. VPNs encrypt data and hide IPs, allowing users to access websites and services that may otherwise be blocked or restricted.
