Q. What are the different types of VPNs? A. The three main types of VPNs are: 1) Remote Access VPNs, which provide a secure connection from one network to another; 2) Site-to-Site VPNs, which connect multiple locations to each other securely; and 3) Tunneling Protocols, which provides secure encryption and control over data.
